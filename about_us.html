About Us!


Our Sweet History


Hailing from our beautiful twin-island state of Trinidad and Tobago, HoneyBee Bakery is as authentic as it gets with all your favourite Trinbagonian baked goods. But every success has its own origin story. HoneyBee started as the unrefined aspirations of 13-year old Maya Brant, who decided at right then and there that she needed to do more with her gift of baking. With traditional recipes passed down from generation to generation of the matriarchal Brant family, Maya was confident she could realize her dream. Starting as a small pop-up shop on Henry Street, Maraval in 2018, Maya grew her business into the own-stop final destination of all baked needs!


Be it a birthday for your little own, a prefessional/catering event, HoneyBee makes all your sweet dreams come true, and with our expansion into becoming an online shopfront, HoneyBee endeavors to supply our customers with a more tailored experience.


Mission Statement

HoneyBee will forever strive to keep the tradition alive by producing high-quality, hand-crafted confectionaries and pastries that evoke sweet, sweet feeling of home and nostalgia with every bite. Our mission is to bake gourmet goodies that inspire joy, foster community, and enrich the life of every customer with warmth and sweetness.
